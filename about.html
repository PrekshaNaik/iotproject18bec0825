Why sustainibility is everything ?

In a world fillled with single use plastic,we want to teach you to consciously recycle more and buy less. After all we have only one home habitable enough for humans!

Learn how to dispose your plasticware through our webservice based on plastic and its 7 grades! Improper disposal of plastic becomes the main reason for pollution in the age of individuality and materialism.Since 1950 to 2018, about 6.3 billion tonnes of plastics have been produced worldwide. Only 9% and 12% of these respectively have been recycled and incinerated.
